Social Campaign Theme

WAJOOD

Embrace Yourself

Introduction

You were born free! Are you now? With the shackles of society binding you, what is freedom? Is it just being free from social stigmas, stereotypes, or social norms? Or is it being able to truly accept yourself for who you are, embracing your identity and creating a place for yourself in this world. This rendezvous, we urge you to join us as we embark on this journey, let’s find out, ‘what’s your wajood?’

An affair with Cliche.
A visit to the House of Stereotype
A Human Being.
A lost Identity?

Humans are social animals;the society grooms them. Sometimes these humans create beliefs that suit them. But when with this time and tide, these beliefs remain as rigid as ever, a STEREOTYPE is born.

The young girl who was made to feel embarrassed for saying 'sanitary napkin' out loud;
The young teenager who was made to take up sciences as a subject;
The blind man who had to finally accept that he was a liability;
The little boy who couldn't have played with Barbies;
This is how deep rooted the ancient stereotype is.
